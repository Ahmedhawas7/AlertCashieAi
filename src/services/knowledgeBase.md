# CARV & Cashie Knowledge Base

## CARV Protocol
CARV is building a modular Data Availability and Identity (DATA) layer for gaming and AI. It empowers users to own, control, and monetize their data while providing brands/developers with high-quality, privacy-preserving data insights.

## Cashie
Cashie is a key component of the CARV ecosystem, often associated with creator campaigns, x402 tokens, and modular social identities. It's designed to facilitate creator engagement and reward community participation.

## CARV ID
CARV ID is a sovereign identity for the gaming world. It aggregates your gaming data and identities across platforms into a single, portable profile.

## veCARV
veCARV is the voting escrow version of the CARV token, used for governance and earning rewards within the ecosystem.

## Nodes & Verifiers
CARV utilizes a decentralized network of verifier nodes to ensure data integrity and security. Node owners are rewarded for their contribution to the network.

## Staking
Users can stake CARV or other ecosystem tokens to participate in governance, earn rewards, and support the network's stability.

## Base Network
The Cashie Watcher bot operates primarily on Base, a secure, low-cost, builder-friendly Ethereum L2 incubated by Coinbase.
