# Base Network & USDC

This bot operates on **Base**, an Ethereum Layer 2 (L2) network incubated by Coinbase. It is designed to be fast, secure, and low-cost.

## USDC on Base
USDC (USD Coin) is a stablecoin pegged 1:1 to the US Dollar. On Base, USDC transactions are extremely cheap (often less than $0.01).

## Benefits
- **Speed**: Transactions are confirmed in seconds.
- **Cost**: No more expensive gas fees.
- **Safety**: Built on the security of Ethereum.

You can send USDC to your friends using their @username or wallet address through this bot.
