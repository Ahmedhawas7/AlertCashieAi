# CARV Protocol Overview

CARV is building the largest modular Data Availability Layer for gaming and AI. It empowers users to own, control, verify, and monetize their data.

## Key Components
- **CARV ID**: Your persistent, cross-platform identity.
- **Data-to-Earn (D2E)**: Earn rewards by sharing your gaming data.
- **CARV Play**: A gaming platform integrated with the CARV ecosystem.

## Vision
To create a future where data is a legacy for individuals, not just corporations.
By using this bot, you are interacting with your CARV identity on the Base network.
