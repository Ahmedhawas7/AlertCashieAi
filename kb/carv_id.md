# CARV ID

The CARV ID is an ERC-7231 NFT that aggregates your multi-chain identity, social accounts, and gaming data into one secure, sovereign identifier.

## Privacy
Your data is encrypted. You decide which data to share with which applications and for what reward.

## Smart Wallets
When you connect your CARV ID to this bot, a smart wallet is utilized on the Base network to execute your transactions securely.

## How to Get One
Visit the [CARV Portal](https://protocol.carv.io) to mint your ID and start aggregating your gaming assets.
